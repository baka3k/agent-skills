# Output Quality Checklist

- Every quantitative claim has a source URL or an explicit assumption marker.
- Estimate output includes best/base/worst PM and cost ranges.
- Fixed-price and T&M assumptions are consistent.
- Staffing plan covers Discovery, Foundation, Build, Stabilize/UAT, Go-live/Hypercare.
- Quality gates include status and remediation notes.
- Proposal is in English by default.
- Slides include a citation section.
