# Evidence Source Ranking

- Tier 1: `mind_mcp` and `graph_mcp` retrieved artifacts.
- Tier 2: Standards and research institutions.
- Tier 3: Vendor or practitioner guides.

Confidence weighting guideline:

- Tier 1 direct match: +0.35
- Tier 2 external standard: +0.20
- Tier 3 supporting context: +0.10

If Tier 1 is missing, confidence cannot exceed Medium.
